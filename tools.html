MidEra's Tools

These are the tools you'll (probably) be using to enhance your experience!

See the Tools
  • ToolBox

    • ToolBox is just the launcher for MidEra. Use this to manage and launch your MidEra tools efficiently, open your projects, and view resources such as documentation.

  • Constructor

    • This app will allow you to make ALL of your materials you will be using in your MidEra games!

  • MPF

    • MPF allows you to compile all of your assets for your MidEra games! MPF stand for MidEra Package Format.

  • img2gif

    • This tool allows you to quickly make .gif files using image sequences. Just input the path to your images, wait a moment, and use your newly created .gif file anywhere!
